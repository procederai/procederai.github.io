Alessandro Flaborea, PhD

Co-founder

Alessandro Flaborea, Ph.D., is an AI innovator currently leading a startup in collaboration with ItalAI.
This venture builds on the cutting-edge research he conducted during his Ph.D., where he focused on revolutionizing AI-powered systems.
His work now drives advancements in areas like procedural learning, video anomaly detection, and generative AI models, positioning his startup at the forefront of AI innovation.
Alessandro completed his Ph.D. at Sapienza University of Rome, where his research explored procedural learning, anomaly detection, hyperbolic neural networks, and large language models.
His work has been featured in leading conferences, including "PREGO: Online Mistake Detection in Procedural Egocentric Videos" (CVPR 2024) and "Hyp2Nav: Hyperbolic Planning for Crowd Navigation" (oral at IROS 2024).
His academic background laid a strong foundation in AI, shaping his expertise in machine learning and computer vision.

Related research

CVPR '24

PREGO

Online mistake detection in
PRocedural EGOcentric videos

Promptly identifying procedural errors from egocentric videos in an online setting is highly challenging and valuable for detecting mistakes as soon as they happen. However, no technique can currently detect open-set procedural mistakes online. We propose PREGO, the first online one-class classification model for mistake detection in PRocedural EGOcentric videos.

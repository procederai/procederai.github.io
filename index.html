Meet the team

Alessandro Flaborea, PhD

Co-founder & CTO

Alessandro Flaborea holds a PhD in Computer Science from Sapienza University of Rome, specializing in Computer Vision. With international research experience at the University of Amsterdam, Alessandro has contributed extensively to the field through publications in top-tier conferences and journals, including CVPR, ICCV, and IROS. Related research

CVPR '24

PREGO

Online mistake detection in
PRocedural EGOcentric videos

Promptly identifying procedural errors from egocentric videos in an online setting is highly challenging and valuable for detecting mistakes as soon as they happen. However, no technique can currently detect open-set procedural mistakes online. We propose PREGO, the first online one-class classification model for mistake detection in PRocedural EGOcentric videos.
